About Us

Who we are?

The Empezar Group was incorporated in 2008 and since then has spread its wings into various elements of the logistics cycle. Empezar has a vision to become a leading integrated logistics solutions provider, on the backbone of a powerful technological platform to deliver customer delight. In a short span, Empezar has built robust relationships with Shipping Lines and Clients alike. We have operations in Mundra & Hazira; and offices at Gandhidham, Mumbai and Delhi.
